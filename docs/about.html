What it is

Study Companion helps students lock in and find the right people to study with. It blends a focused lofi study room, lightweight profiles, and class-based matching.

  • Study Room: clean lofi ambience plus a simple timer.
  • Find Study Buddies: match by school, classes, and preferences.
  • Profiles: public/private visibility with optional contact info.

How matching works

Submitting your preferences generates a match score (1–5) for each candidate:

  • Shared classes (highest weight; multiple overlaps boost score)
  • Study style alignment (e.g., quiet vs. collaborative)
  • Time preference (morning / afternoon / evening)

Only public profiles appear; users can opt out of DMs.

Status

Private Messaging: demo-only (local, non-realtime, no cross-device sync).

Study Spots: proof-of-concept UI (not backed by live data yet).

Tech & hosting
